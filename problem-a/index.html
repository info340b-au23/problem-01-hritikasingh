About SpongeBob SquarePants

SpongeBob SquarePants, voiced by Tom Kenny, was and continues to be a popular character from the children's show, SpongeBob SquarePants. The show has been on air for the last 24 years, since July 17th, 1999. SpongeBob has a tight knit circle including Patrick, Squidward, Mr. Crabs, Mrs. Puff, and of course, Gary.
